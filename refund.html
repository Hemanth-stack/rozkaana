Free Trial Policy

All new Rozkaana accounts receive a 7-day free trial with full access to their chosen plan. No credit card is required to start the trial.

WHAT THIS MEANS

At the end of your 7-day trial, absolutely nothing happens. We do not charge you automatically. We do not ask for card details. Your trial simply ends and Meal Plan generation pauses. To continue, visit the Billing page and subscribe.

There is nothing to “refund” during a trial because no payment is collected. The 7-day trial is not a paid period and is not subject to the money-back guarantee below.

✅

7-Day Money-Back Guarantee (First Paid Period)

If you subscribe and are not satisfied with your Meal Plans within the first 7 days of your first paid subscription, we will issue a full refund — no questions asked.

ELIGIBLE FOR REFUND

First-time paid subscribers who request a refund within 7 calendar days of their first payment date. The refund covers the full amount charged, including GST.

NOT ELIGIBLE

Refund requests after 7 days of the first payment; renewals and subsequent billing cycles; plan upgrades or downgrades; accounts terminated for Terms of Service violations; duplicate accounts created to claim a second trial or guarantee.

The 7-day money-back guarantee applies once per person (based on email address and payment method). Creating multiple accounts to claim multiple guarantees is a violation of our Terms of Service and will result in account termination.

🔄

Subscription Renewals

After the first paid period, your subscription renews monthly automatically. We send a renewal reminder email 3 days before each billing date.

NOT REFUNDABLE

Renewal payments are non-refundable. If you do not want to be billed for the next cycle, you must cancel your subscription before your renewal date from the Billing page.

When you cancel, your access continues until the end of the period you have already paid for. We do not prorate or partially refund unused days in a renewal cycle.

Exception — Duplicate Billing: If you were charged twice for the same billing period due to a technical error, we will refund the duplicate charge in full immediately upon verification.

⏸️

Pausing vs Cancelling

If you need a break but plan to come back, pausing is better than cancelling:

  • Pause for up to 30 days per year from the Billing page
  • No charge during the pause period — your billing cycle extends by the pause duration
  • Your profile, preferences, and history are preserved
  • Resume any time and Meal Plans restart the next morning

Cancelling ends your subscription at the close of the current billing period. You will not be charged again. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it may be deleted in accordance with our Privacy Policy.

📝

How to Request a Refund

1
Email us at support@rozkaana.in from the email address registered to your account. Subject line: “Refund Request.”
2
Include in your email: your registered email address, the date of payment, the amount charged, and (optional but helpful) a reason for your request. No lengthy explanation required.
3
We confirm within 24 hours. We will verify your eligibility and confirm the refund by email. You do not need to do anything else.
4
Credit appears in 5–7 business days. Refunds are processed via Razorpay to your original payment method — UPI, card, or net banking. Timing depends on your bank’s processing speed.

For urgent or complex refund queries, contact us via WhatsApp. Our support team operates Monday to Saturday, 9 AM – 9 PM IST.

🔔

Special Circumstances

Service Outages

If the Service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours due to our infrastructure failure (excluding planned maintenance with prior notice), we will issue a prorated credit for the affected days. This credit is applied to your next billing cycle, not issued as a cash refund.

Medical Circumstances

If you are unable to use the Service due to a medical emergency (hospitalisation or similar) after your 7-day window, contact us with documentation. We review these cases individually and may issue a credit or extend your subscription by the affected period.

Billing Errors

If you believe you were charged an incorrect amount, contact us within 30 days of the charge. We investigate and correct all verified billing errors promptly.
