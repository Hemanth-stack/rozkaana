Getting Started
Click “Start Free Trial” on the homepage. Enter your email address and we’ll send you a 6-digit OTP to verify. Once verified, you’ll be taken through a 4-step onboarding wizard to set up your profile — this takes about 4 minutes. Your 7-day free trial starts immediately after onboarding is complete. No credit card is required to start.
We ask for: your name, age, gender, current weight (kg), and height (cm) — these are used to calculate your BMI and daily calorie/macro targets. Then your health goal (weight loss, maintenance, or muscle gain), any health conditions (diabetes, PCOS, hypertension, etc.), and food allergies. Finally, your dietary preference (veg, non-veg, Jain, Sattvic) and up to 3 regional cuisine preferences. That’s it.
Your first plan is generated the night after you complete onboarding and will be delivered to your email at 6:00 AM the following morning. If you onboard tonight, you’ll have your first plan in your inbox by 6 AM tomorrow. You can also generate today’s plan immediately from your dashboard if you’re on the Pro or Family plan.
Check your spam/junk folder first — our emails sometimes land there on first delivery. Add menu@rozkaana.in to your contacts to avoid this in future. If you’re on the Pro plan, you can also trigger a plan manually from the “My Plan” page by clicking “Generate Now.” If the issue persists, contact us at support@rozkaana.in.
Meal Plans & Customisation
Go to Settings → Food Preferences → Cuisine Preferences. Select up to 3 cuisines (on Pro and Family plans) or 1 cuisine (on Starter). Save your changes. Your next plan — delivered the following morning — will use your updated preferences. You can also override just today’s cuisine from the dashboard using the “↻ Regen” button on Pro plans.
Yes. On the “My Plan” page, each meal slot has a small ✕ button. Clicking it removes that meal from today’s plan. Skipped meals don’t carry over to tomorrow — your next day’s plan is freshly generated. To permanently avoid a type of dish (e.g., you dislike sabudana), update your Food Preferences in Settings or note it in your profile conditions.
Our system tracks every dish you’ve received in the past 7 days and excludes those from tomorrow’s plan. This applies separately to each meal slot — so if you had Rajma Chawal for lunch on Monday, it won’t appear in your lunch slot again until at least next Tuesday. The pool of 5,000+ recipes ensures we can maintain this variety indefinitely.
Yes, if you’re on the Pro or Family plan. From the “My Plan” page, click the “↻ Regen” button. Regeneration takes about 2 minutes. You can also request a specific cuisine for the regenerated plan. Starter plan members receive their plan at 6 AM and cannot regenerate — upgrading to Pro unlocks this feature.
On the “My Plan” page, click the “🛒 Grocery” button. This opens a printable grocery list for today’s meals, scaled to the number of people in your household. A weekly consolidated grocery list is also included in your Friday morning PDF. Household members on the Family plan get a combined grocery list for all members.
Health & Nutrition
Yes. When you select “Diabetes T2” in your health conditions, our engine filters recipes using glycemic index (GI) data. High-GI foods are excluded or portioned carefully. Meals are structured to avoid large carbohydrate spikes — for example, rice portions are moderated, and refined flour is replaced with whole grain alternatives. We recommend consulting your doctor about target calorie levels alongside using Rozkaana.
For PCOS, our system prioritises low-GI foods, anti-inflammatory ingredients (turmeric, flaxseed, leafy greens common in Indian cooking), and adequate protein to support hormonal balance. High-sugar foods and refined carbs are deprioritised. Iron-rich dishes are included to address the anemia that often co-occurs with PCOS. You can log wellness signals like “energy dips” and “cramps” daily, and our AI uses these signals to adapt your plan over time.
Wellness Signals are daily health check-ins on the “My Plan” page. You log your energy level (1–10), sleep quality (1–10), digestion comfort (1–10), and optional flags like hair loss, muscle cramps, or blood sugar dips. After 5+ days of data, our system identifies nutritional patterns — for example, consistently low energy might indicate iron deficiency — and adjusts your plan to address them. Logging takes under 30 seconds daily.

Account & Billing
Nothing happens automatically — we don’t take any payment at the end of the trial because no credit card is required to start. If you’d like to continue, visit the Billing page and choose a plan. Your meal plans pause if you don’t subscribe after the trial ends.
Go to Billing and select the plan you want. Upgrades take effect immediately — you’ll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. You won’t lose access to your current plan features until the cycle ends.
From the Billing page, click “Pause.” Your subscription pauses immediately — no more plans will be generated during the pause period. You can pause for up to 30 days per year, and your billing period extends by the paused duration. Resume any time from the same page. Pausing more than 30 days in a calendar year is not available — you’d need to cancel and resubscribe.
Go to Billing → click “Cancel Subscription.” Your access continues until the end of the current billing period — we don’t terminate access immediately. No cancellation fees apply. If you cancel in the first 7 days of a paid subscription (not trial), you can request a full refund. See our Refund Policy for details.
We accept all major Indian payment methods via Razorpay: UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), Credit and Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), Net Banking (all major Indian banks), and Wallets. International cards are supported on a best-effort basis. All transactions are processed in Indian Rupees (INR) and include GST where applicable.
Rozkaana uses passwordless login via OTP — there is no password to update. To change your email address, contact us at support@rozkaana.in with your current and new email, and we’ll update it after verifying ownership of both.
Household & Family
You must be on the Family plan. Go to the Household page and click “Invite Member.” This generates a unique invite link valid for 48 hours. Share the link with your family member — they click it, sign in with their own account (or create one), and are added to your household. Each member sets up their own health profile during onboarding. The Family plan supports up to 6 members total.
Yes. Each member’s plan is personalised to their individual profile — their own calorie targets, health conditions, and dietary preferences. The household plan is coordinated so that overlapping meals (meals the household can share) are highlighted, and the grocery list is consolidated. However, if one member has diabetes and another is a gym-goer, their plans will be meaningfully different.
If you downgrade from Family to a lower plan, household members will lose access at the end of your current billing period. They will be notified by email in advance. Members can create individual accounts on their own plan. We recommend notifying your members before downgrading.
Technical & App
First check your spam/junk folder and search for emails from menu@rozkaana.in. If it’s there, mark it as “Not Spam” to ensure future delivery. Add menu@rozkaana.in to your contacts. If you’re on the Pro plan, you can also view and download your plan directly from the My Plan dashboard. If you haven’t received any emails in 2+ days, contact us at support@rozkaana.in.
PDFs are generated after your meal plan — this usually takes 5–10 minutes after plan delivery. If the button says “PDF generating…”, wait a few minutes and refresh the page. If it’s been more than 30 minutes and the PDF still hasn’t appeared, contact us at support@rozkaana.in with the date of your plan and we’ll regenerate it manually.
Go to Settings → scroll to the “Danger Zone” section → click “Delete My Account.” A confirmation dialog will appear. Once confirmed, your account, all meal plans, health data, and payment records are permanently deleted from our systems within 7 business days. This action cannot be undone. If you have an active subscription, it is cancelled immediately on deletion.
