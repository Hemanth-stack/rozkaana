Why We Exist

The gap between knowing
and eating well

Most diet platforms were designed for the West. We built Rozkaana to solve the specific problems Indian bodies, Indian kitchens, and Indian budgets actually face.

The Challenge

Nutritionists cost ₹2,000–₹10,000 per month

A service accessible to fewer than 3% of Indian households, leaving the rest without structured, personalised guidance.

Western apps don’t speak Indian food

They recommend chicken breasts and kale. They have no concept of poha, dal fry, or a Gujarati thali’s nutritional balance.

Repetition kills adherence

Eating the same dal rice every day causes the majority of Indian dieters to abandon their plan within two weeks.

Households cook one meal for everyone

Individual goals — weight loss, diabetes management, muscle gain — get lost when the whole family eats the same food.

Our Answer

Full personalisation at ₹199/month

95% cheaper than a human nutritionist. Every plan is built from scratch using your BMI, health conditions, and macro targets — not a template.

5,000+ FSSAI-verified Indian recipes

Built across 12 regional cuisines by certified Indian dieticians. North Indian, South Indian, Bengali, Gujarati, Odia — never translated from Western templates.

7-day non-repeat variety engine

No dish appears twice in a rolling week. A rotating pool of 5,000+ recipes ensures your plan always feels fresh and satisfying.

Household mode for up to 6 members

Each member gets their own health profile, macro targets, and eating mode. One plan, every constraint respected, one consolidated grocery list.
