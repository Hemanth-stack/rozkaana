Our Story

India actually needed.

Every other app was designed for a Western body eating Western food. We started Rozkaana because no one else was solving the right problem.

How We Started

It started with a nutritionist and a frustrated customer.

In 2023, Priya — a certified sports nutritionist — was fielding the same question from every client: “Why can’t I get a plan that actually uses the food my family cooks?”

Existing apps recommended quinoa, kale, and Greek yoghurt to people who grew up on dal, chawal, and dahi. The few Indian apps that existed gave the same 10-recipe rotation to everyone, regardless of whether you were a diabetic grandmother in Chennai or a gym-going student in Pune.

So Priya partnered with a team of engineers and built Rozkaana: a system that could understand Indian food, Indian health conditions, and Indian bodies — and generate a genuinely personalised plan from thousands of verified Indian recipes. Not a template. A real plan.

Our Mission

Make expert-level nutrition accessible to every Indian household.

A personalised meal plan from a qualified nutritionist costs ₹2,000–₹10,000 per month. That makes expert guidance inaccessible to 97% of Indian households. Rozkaana changes that ratio.

At ₹199/month, we believe structured, personalised, science-backed nutrition should not be a luxury. It should be as ordinary as checking the weather. Our north star: ≥70% weekly plan adherence at day 30 — because a plan that people actually follow is the only plan that works.

Our Approach

Why Rozkaana is different from every app you’ve tried.

Indian food, first.
